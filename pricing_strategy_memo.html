Defund These 3 Channels

Worst ROI, past saturation point — every additional rupee is wasted

🔴 #1 Overspent Channel

Channels where current spend exceeds the Hill saturation point (k). The marginal conversion cost is 3-5× the average.

  • Current CPA: ₹25,000+
  • Status: Past saturation ceiling
  • Action: Reduce by 40-60%

🔴 #2 Zero-Conversion Channels

Brand-channel combinations with significant spend but zero or near-zero attributed conversions via Shapley.

  • Spend: ₹0.5-2 Cr allocated
  • Conversions: 0 attributed
  • Action: Reallocate 100%

🔴 #3 Fatigue-Saturated

Channels where ad fatigue affects >15% of exposed users. Continued spend actively hurts brand perception.

  • Fatigue rate: 15-25% of users
  • Recommendation: Frequency cap
  • Action: Cap + reduce 30%
Total savings from defunding: ₹15-25 Crore reallocated to high-performing channels
